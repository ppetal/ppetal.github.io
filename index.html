About Platform Petal

Launching You To New Heights

Platform Petal Inc. solves and provides for all the needs of newly found start-ups. We are a dedicated team of young entrepreneurs who handle all the tasks ranging from Web development, Marketing, Business, Sponsorship, Research and Development to foster the growth of an idea into a successful venture. We at Platform Petal believe in your ideas and your aspirations. Our team will whole heartedly try and help you in implementing your dream and making it come true. We are a bunch of web developers, writers, managers and graphic designers who work relentlessly to make your startup happen. Let the world not be devoid of any brilliant idea. Let there always be a petal for every idea.
